Why Us


Approximately 20% of Medicare patients are readmitted into the hospital within 30 days post initial discharge. At Continued Care Partners we strive to minimize readmission rates by delivering remarkable transitional care. Our company boasts a 94% success rate in preventing readmissions by completing timely post discharge evaluations. In addition, we focus on supporting our patients through continued self management and education of their health condition.
